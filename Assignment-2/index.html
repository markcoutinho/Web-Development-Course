CRYPTO

Crypto is a legend that was released in Apex Legends Season 3.
Mystik -- "I survived my first match, with only two broken ribs. Being as safe as I can, and keeping my distance.
Unfortunately, the others are already asking about the Tower. The one Legend you love confronted me on the dropship.
I thought he had evidence, but it turns out he’s just an idiot.
Crypto specializes in secrets; he knows how to uncover them, and how to keep them. A brilliant hacker and encryption expert,
he uses aerial drones to spy on his opponents in the Apex Arena without being seen.
It’s been noted that his drones have a similar design to those created by wanted murderer Tae Joon Park.

Character Abilities

  • Tactical Ability - Surveillance Drone
  • Passive Ability - Neurolink
  • Ultimate Ability - Drone EMP
  • Transform Ability - Face Clone
The drone can be deployed and flown up to 200 meters away from Crypto. If the drone is 240+ meters away from Crypto, it'll automatically be recalled.
